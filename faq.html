Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about tutoring with Brightmind. Can't find your answer? Get in touch.

Who are your tutors?

+

Our tutors are current medical and university students from Québec's top institutions like McGill, Université de Montréal and Concordia, alongside standout CEGEP students in enriched programs. Every tutor is vetted for both subject mastery and the ability to teach it clearly.

What subjects do you cover?

+

We focus on STEM: mathematics and calculus, physics, chemistry and biology, spanning high school and CEGEP all the way through complex university-level coursework.

How are sessions held?

+

All sessions are one-on-one and online over video call, so you can learn from anywhere on a schedule that works for you. Every session is prepared in advance around your syllabus and goals.

How much does it cost?

+

Your first 30-minute consultation is completely free. After that, 1-on-1 subject tutoring is $50 per session, and our academic & lifestyle coaching is $60 per session. See the full breakdown here.

What makes Brightmind different?

+

We teach you how to learn, not just what to learn. Alongside the subject material, our mentors coach you on study methods, time management and the lifestyle habits that make academic success sustainable, because intelligence is a skill, not a fixed trait.

How do I get started?

+

Book your free consultation through the contact form. We'll assess your needs, identify your goals, and pair you with the tutor who fits best.

Can I switch tutors?

+

Absolutely, at any time and at no extra cost. The right fit matters, and we'll happily re-match you until it feels right.

Do you offer packages or discounts?

+

Yes, we offer multi-session packages for students committing to regular support. Reach out via the contact form and we'll put together a plan that fits.

Still have questions?

Book a free intro call and we'll walk you through everything.
