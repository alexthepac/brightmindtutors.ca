FOR MEDICAL SCHOOL APPLICANTS

CASPer & MMI Interview Preparation

The CASPer situational-judgment test and the Multiple Mini Interview are the single most decisive stage of a medical school application. Most schools weigh them as heavily as your GPA, and every year strong students with top grades are turned away on interview day. Our mentors earned acceptances to all four Québec medical schools, and they coach you through realistic timed scenarios, proven answer frameworks, and honest feedback until you stay calm and sharp under pressure.
